About Tenacity Locks Ltd

Our Story

From Hardware Shop to Kenya's Trusted Lock Brand

Tenacity Locks Ltd was born out of a simple observation: Kenyan homeowners and businesses deserved better quality, more reliable door security products than what was commonly available in the market.

We started in 2011 as S.K. Hardware — a small hardware outlet on Kijabe Street, Nairobi. By 2012, we had formally incorporated as Tenacity Locks Ltd with a clear, singular focus: door locks and security hardware.

Over the decade that followed, we built deep relationships with world-class manufacturers, refined our product range, and grew to serve customers from Mombasa to Kisumu, Nairobi to Garissa — all 47 counties of Kenya.

Today, Tenacity is synonymous with quality, reliability, and value in the Kenyan door security market. Our 5-year guarantee, CE-certified products, and non-duplicatable key technology set us apart from the competition.
